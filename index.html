The Magnificent Ishtar Gate

Explore a 3D model of the ancient Ishtar Gate from Babylon. Constructed around 575 BCE by order of King Nebuchadnezzar II, it was the eighth gate to the inner city of Babylon. It was famed for its glazed bricks depicting formidable dragons and majestic aurochs, serving as a vibrant entrance to the city's processional way.
